About the platform

              Welcome to our 3D models platform! Here you will find a vast collection of three-dimensional models of household objects, weapons, external assets, plants and vegetables, architecture, and much more.

              The platform was created with the aim of solving a problem: people who want to develop games or projects involving 3D objects, especially independent developers or small companies, face difficulties when it comes to creating a game project. They need to program, model, and texture, but often face financial constraints that make it unfeasible to purchase assets ranging from $1 to $50. Thus, they are faced with the decision to spend money without knowing if they will have a return or to model and texture on their own. In my case, I went through this situation, working hard and going back to square one several times due to the constant learning of new model optimization techniques. The third option is simply to give up on the project.

             In the face of these challenges, I decided to create a channel and a website to help solve some of these problems. The channel is aimed at people who model games or elements within them, and who need knowledge and techniques to enhance their work. Our platform offers original 3D models in low-poly format to be enhanced with Blender's subdivision surface or other 3D software. This is the version where you can increase or decrease the polygon count as needed. We also provide ready-made low-poly versions for those who just want to download and use them without modifications, as well as the median-poly version, ready for use as well. All models are free and unrestricted under the CC0 license. This means you can use, modify, and distribute these models freely, including for commercial purposes, without the need for permission or attribution.

             Our goal is to provide a wide variety of high-quality 3D models to facilitate the work of entrepreneurs, companies, and indie developers. We hope that you find the models you need and enjoy our platform.
